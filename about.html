Who We Are

Our Story

696 DEPT is more than a brand. It's a statement. Inspired by street culture, built for creatives and dreamers.

We create pieces that speak loud without saying a word.

Born in Gaborone, Botswana — we carry the spirit of a generation that refuses to blend in. Every stitch, every graphic, every drop is intentional.

Mission

To build timeless streetwear that empowers self-expression and connects culture. We design for those who live loudly, who turn everyday streets into runways, and who understand that fashion is identity.
