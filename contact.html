FAQ

How long does delivery take? +
Standard delivery takes 3–5 business days within Gaborone and surrounding areas. Express delivery takes 1–2 business days. You'll receive an order confirmation and tracking details via email.
What sizes do you carry? +
We carry sizes S, M, L, XL, and XXL. Our tees are designed with a relaxed streetwear fit. Please refer to our size guide for measurements.
Can I return or exchange an item? +
Yes. We accept returns and exchanges within 14 days of delivery, provided the item is unused and in original condition. Contact us at info@696dept.com to arrange a return.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept credit/debit cards, EFT/bank transfers, Orange Money, and Mascom MyZaka. All payments are processed securely.
Are your products limited edition? +
Some of our drops are limited edition with restricted quantities. Sign up to our newsletter and follow us on social media to be notified of new drops before they sell out.
